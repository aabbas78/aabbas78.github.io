About Naktal

NAKTAL has been in the field of Identification and Product Protection since 1996 and has grown into a leading company in the field. NAKTAL is committed to serving the needs of multi-domain companies that require security seals on their products and assets. We market a wide range of security tags and seals that are used by a wide range of different industries such as: airlines, logistics and shipping companies to reduce tampering and provide evidence of tampering. NAKTAL's reputation is built on the company's commitment to providing the highest quality of both products and services and its time commitment.
